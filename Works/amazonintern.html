Amazon SDE Internship

I was a Software Development Engineer Intern at Amazon Inc., specifically in Amazon Robotics Robin Perception team.

Robin is this robot arm responsible for recognizing where each package should go and assigning them to the correct delivery bin. As an intern, my project was related to creating a tool that will allow the analyzation of all of Robin's data from its failure to its successes.

I first had a design process overview with the team engineers to get their feedbacks. Then, AWS tools, S3, EMR, and SageMaker, were used to complete my internship.

I was able to speed up the process of the data analyzation by 10 times from the previous tool that the team already had. The details and a demo of my completed project was presented to the team at the end of the internship.
